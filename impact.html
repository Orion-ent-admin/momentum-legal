Our Story

Momentum was built by a United States Marine Corps veteran living with Multiple Sclerosis. What started as a personal tool to manage daily habits and routines became something bigger — an app designed to help anyone build the structure and consistency that makes life more manageable.

MS affects nearly 1 million people in the United States. There is no cure, but research is making real progress every year. We believe that even small, consistent contributions can drive meaningful change — the same philosophy behind the app itself.

That's why we commit 10% of all net revenue to the National Multiple Sclerosis Society. Not sometimes. Every time. It's part of who we are.
Forest Konwerski Founder, Momentum · USMC Veteran
