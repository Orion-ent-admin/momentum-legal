Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: Momentum is designed with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Momentum, developed by Orion Column ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS mobile application ("the App").

By using Momentum, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection and Storage

2.1 What Data is Created

When you use Momentum, you create:

  • Habits: Your custom habit titles, schedules, colors, and descriptions
  • Routines: Your routine steps, schedules, and completion records
  • Goals: Your goal titles, progress data, and milestones
  • Completion Records: Timestamps of when you complete habits and routines
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, and app configuration

2.2 Where Your Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

  • ✅ Your data never leaves your device
  • ✅ We do not have servers or databases
  • ✅ We cannot access your data
  • ✅ Your data is protected by your device's security (encryption, Face ID, etc.)

2.3 What We DON'T Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • ❌ Personal identifiable information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Photos or media from your device
  • ❌ Contacts or calendar information
  • ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data
  • ❌ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ❌ Any health or fitness data

3. How We Use Information

Since all data stays on your device, we don't use your information. Your data is used solely by the App on your device to:

  • Display your habits, routines, and goals
  • Track your completion history and streaks
  • Generate progress reports and statistics
  • Send local notifications (if you enable them)
  • Populate widgets on your home screen

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data with anyone. Period.

  • ✅ No third-party analytics services
  • ✅ No advertising networks
  • ✅ No data brokers
  • ✅ No marketing partners
  • ✅ No cloud sync services

Exception - Apple Services: We use Apple's built-in frameworks for:

  • Subscriptions: Apple processes payments through the App Store (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Notifications: Apple's notification system (if you enable notifications)
  • Widgets: Apple's WidgetKit for displaying data on your home screen

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights and Data Control

5.1 Access Your Data

You have complete access to all your data at any time through the App interface.

5.2 Delete Your Data

You can delete your data in two ways:

  • Individual items: Delete any habit, routine, or goal from within the App
  • All data: Go to Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the App permanently removes all data from your device
