Your Privacy Matters: Momentum is designed with privacy at its core. Your data is stored on your device and optionally synced across your devices using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit). We do not operate our own servers, and we cannot read or access your synced data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Momentum, developed by Orion Column ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS and watchOS application ("the App").

By using Momentum, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection and Storage

2.1 What Data is Created

When you use Momentum, you create:

  • Habits: Your custom habit titles, schedules, colors, and descriptions
  • Routines: Your routine steps, schedules, and completion records
  • Goals: Your goal titles, progress data, and milestones
  • Events: Your scheduled events, categories, and reminders
  • Daily Briefs: Your daily notes and reflections
  • Completion Records: Timestamps of when you complete habits and routines
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, and app configuration

2.1.1 Sign in with Apple

Momentum offers optional Sign in with Apple for account authentication. When you sign in, Apple may provide us with:

  • Name: Your first and last name (only provided on first sign-in; you may choose to hide it)
  • Email: Your email address or an Apple-generated private relay address
  • User Identifier: A unique, anonymous identifier tied to your Apple Account

This information is stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud (see Section 2.2). We do not store this information on any servers we operate.

2.2 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and automatically synced across your devices via Apple's iCloud (CloudKit).

  • ✅ Your data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud account
  • ✅ iCloud data is stored in your private CloudKit database — only accessible by devices signed into your Apple Account
  • ✅ We do not operate servers or databases — syncing is handled entirely by Apple
  • ✅ We cannot access your synced data
  • ✅ Your data is protected by Apple's iCloud encryption and your device's security (passcode, Face ID, etc.)

iCloud sync requires an active Apple Account with iCloud enabled. If you disable iCloud for Momentum, your data will remain stored locally on your device only.

2.3 What We DON'T Collect

We do not collect, transmit to our own servers, or have access to:

  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Photos or media from your device
  • ❌ Contacts or calendar information
  • ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data
  • ❌ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ❌ Any health or fitness data

3. How We Use Information

Since your data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud account, we don't use your information. Your data is used solely by the App on your devices to:

  • Display your habits, routines, goals, and events
  • Track your completion history and streaks
  • Generate progress reports and statistics
  • Send local notifications (if you enable them)
  • Populate widgets on your home screen and Apple Watch
  • Sync your data across your iPhone and Apple Watch via iCloud

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data with anyone. Period.

  • ✅ No third-party analytics services
  • ✅ No advertising networks
  • ✅ No data brokers
  • ✅ No marketing partners
  • ✅ No third-party cloud services (only Apple's iCloud)

Apple Services: We use Apple's built-in frameworks and services for:

  • iCloud (CloudKit): Syncs your data across your devices using your private iCloud account. Data is stored in Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Sign in with Apple: Optional account authentication (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Subscriptions: Apple processes payments through the App Store (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Notifications: Apple's notification system (if you enable notifications)
  • Widgets: Apple's WidgetKit for displaying data on your home screen and Apple Watch

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights and Data Control

5.1 Access Your Data

You have complete access to all your data at any time through the App interface.

5.2 Delete Your Data

You can delete your data in several ways:

  • Individual items: Delete any habit, routine, goal, or event from within the App
  • All data: Go to Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the App removes all local data from your device
  • iCloud data: To remove synced data from iCloud, go to your device's Settings → Apple Account → iCloud → Manage Storage → Momentum, or disable iCloud sync for the App

5.3 Data Portability & Export

Momentum provides data export features that allow you to download your personal tracking data in JSON and CSV formats (reference "6. Data Export & User Responsibility.").
